## Objectives
- Improve data quality by handling foreign characters, removing duplicates, and converting incorrect data types  
- Enhance data structure through normalization and separation of multi-value columns into dedicated tables  
- Populate missing values to ensure dataset completeness and reliability  
- Perform in-depth SQL analysis to extract meaningful and actionable insights  

---

## Scope
The project covers the full data analysis lifecycle using SQL, including:
- Data collection and understanding  
- Data cleaning and preprocessing  
- Data normalization and table structuring  
- Exploratory and business-focused analysis  

The analysis addresses key business questions related to content production, audience preferences, and operational efficiency.

---

## Key Analysis Areas
- Genre popularity across regions  
- Director productivity and influence on content diversity  
- Content duration trends by genre  
- Regional content preferences and viewing patterns  

---

## Business Impact

### Enhanced Content Relevance
Identifying region-specific genre preferences (for example, high popularity of comedy content in the United States) helps tailor content acquisition strategies, improving viewer engagement and satisfaction.

### Strategic Partnerships
Analyzing prolific and impactful directors enables Netflix to collaborate strategically, increasing content diversity and appealing to broader audience segments.

### Operational Efficiency
Optimizing content length based on genre trends and viewer attention patterns enhances consumption experience and supports higher viewer retention.

### Informed Decision-Making
Data-driven insights into genre performance and directorial impact support better resource allocation, strategic planning, and sustained competitive advantage.
